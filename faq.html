Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about the FIFA World Cup, answered.

When is the next FIFA World Cup?

The FIFA World Cup is held every four years, bringing together the best national teams from across the globe for a month-long celebration of soccer.

How many teams and matches are in the World Cup?

In the current format, a total of 32 teams play in the tournament, competing in a total of 64 matches to decide the champion.

Who is considered the best player in World Cup history?

Many experts and fans consider Pelé to be the best history FIFA player, as he is the only person to have won three World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970).

How does the knockout stage work?

After the group stage, the top teams advance to a single-elimination bracket. If a match is tied after 90 minutes, it goes to extra time and, if necessary, a penalty shootout.

What is the "Golden Boot"?

The Golden Boot is the prestigious award given to the top goalscorer of the FIFA World Cup tournament. It is one of the most coveted individual prizes in sports.

Where can I watch the matches?

FIFA World Cup matches are broadcast globally across various networks and streaming platforms. Check your local listings or the official FIFA website for the most up-to-date broadcast schedules for your region.
